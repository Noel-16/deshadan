About Deshadan

A legacy of hospitality excellence spanning over two decades, rooted in authentic South Indian culture and modern luxury.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Deshadan Resorts & Hotels began as a vision to showcase the authentic beauty and culture of South India through exceptional hospitality. What started as a single property has grown into a collection of premium resorts and heritage hotels across Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Karnataka.

Our name "Deshadan" reflects our commitment to being guardians of our land and culture, preserving traditions while embracing modern luxury. Each property in our portfolio tells a unique story of its destination, offering guests an immersive experience that goes beyond accommodation.

Today, we are proud to be recognized as one of South India's leading hospitality groups, known for our authentic experiences, sustainable practices, and unwavering commitment to guest satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be South India's most respected hospitality brand, creating unforgettable experiences that celebrate local culture, preserve natural heritage, and set new standards for sustainable luxury tourism.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, luxury experiences that connect guests with the soul of South India while supporting local communities and preserving the environment for future generations.
